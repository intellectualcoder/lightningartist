Sanctuary of Peace [NOW SOLD]

"A Sanctuary of Peace" was sold for $750 at a silent auction held at the River Oaks Mosque in Houston. This artwork was generously donated to the mosque by Fatima A., who purchased it and chose to support the mosque with her donation. The sale of this piece will contribute 60% of the proceeds to HEAL Palestine, "an organization committed to providing a brighter future for young Palestinians in Gaza through health, education, aid, and leadership initiatives".
