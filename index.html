Sanctuary of Peace [NOW SOLD]

"A Sanctuary of Peace" was sold for $750 at a silent auction held at the River Oaks Mosque in Houston. The sale of this piece will contribute 60% of the proceeds to HEAL Palestine, "an organization committed to providing a brighter future for young Palestinians in Gaza through health, education, aid, and leadership initiatives".
