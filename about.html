About Fatima

Fatima is a passionate local artist based in Houston, TX, known for creating awe-inspiring modern Islamic art. Her work sends a positive and powerful message about spirituality, bridging the gap between the material world and the spiritual realm through the lens of Islamic teachings.

She enjoys experimenting with various mediums, with a current focus on mixed media and 3-dimensional sculptural pieces. Her work has the unique ability to connect people of all ages and backgrounds, sparking meaningful conversations, deep reflections, and heart-to-heart connections. Fatima believes art can be a transformative tool to inspire dialogue and bridge cultural gaps, encouraging everyone to explore spirituality in a creative and accessible way.

With her vibrant, innovative approach, Fatima aims to bring the beauty and depth of Islamic values into modern spaces, leaving a lasting impression that transcends boundaries and touches the hearts of those who experience her work.
